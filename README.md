# random-agent
Random User-Agent generator written in GoLang

Quickly switch up your user-agent header to evade detection 
small little program that allows you to easily specify whether you want a mobile user-agent or a desktop user-agent generated. 
has a help menu to explain the flag options. 
might not be too impressive but its super convient to just type a one-liner in and boom!
you can also expand your available user-agent lists to your liking.

Happy Hunting Y'all!
